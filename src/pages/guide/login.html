WordPress Login URL

Your WordPress login URL should have been provided to you. If you can't remember it, please reach out. However, it is usually http://yourdomain.com/wp-admin

Username and Password

Your username and password should have been provided by your agency. Do not share your username and password. If you forget your password, you may reset it at the WordPress login page.
